About

I recently completed my Ph.D. under the direction of Jan Prins in the Department of Computer Science at UNC. I was also a trainee of the UNC Big Data 2 Knowledge program (BD2K) and the Curriculum in Bioinformatics and Computational Biology (BCB). My research interests lie in the field of computational biology. For my dissertation, I developed algorithms to decipher dynamics of gene expression using RNA sequencing data. Most recently, I have focused on methods for analysis of single cell RNA-seq data. I joined the lab of Evan Macosko at the Broad Institute as a postdoctoral fellow in August 2017.
